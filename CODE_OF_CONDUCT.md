# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This is an open, welcoming space. Contributors and community members are expected to be respectful and constructive.

## Standards

✅ **Acceptable behavior:**
- Constructive feedback and suggestions
- Respectful disagreement
- Focusing on what is best for the project

❌ **Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Spam or off-topic promotion
- Any form of discrimination

## Enforcement

Issues violating this code of conduct may be closed without response. Repeated violations will result in being blocked from the repository.
